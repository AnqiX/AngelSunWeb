Being Alive

28 Sep 2017

Being Alive was written one year ago after a trip to Galveston with my roomate. Sitting on the beach and listening to the sound of the water makes me feel calm and relaxed. Starring at the horizon beyond the sea, I thought about many things: life, happiness, lonliness...And that was the inspiration of this song.
